Time and Habits Blog

07/04/2017

Which timebox and habits techniques did I try? I am familiar with meditation, mindfulness and flow concepts. Some of the other time management tools I have come across in similar form before. I have been attempting to bring a growth mindset into my days this week.

What did I find interesting? The Pomodoro Technique appealed to me as a very accessable/useful tool and I found the Paid to Exist "Counterintuitive" advice very interesting.

What worked for you? Meditation and mindfulness has been my friend for years. Some of the advice on Flow was useful in making that more accessable as a tool in my mind, particularly the differentiation from "Hyperfocus".

Which will I experiment further with? I am keen to try the Pomodoro technique as a regular tool, and the Fogg method. Whether or not I maintain these as specific tools/concepts or adapt them as I see fit I do not know. :)
